ABOUT US

We are an NGO striving hard to help poor and bring smiles on their faces. It's an absolute non-profit NGO where eople can willingly donate money to help under-privileged people.
The amount can be as minimal as possible, we will make sure that it goes to the deserved poeple.
With the hit of COVID-19, many refugees and internally displaced people have been pushed further into poverty and despair. Struggling to survive, they are faced with impossible choices. By donating to "HAND TO HELP ", by Sparks Foundation, we can make sure that refugees are not alone. We support refugees and poor people to access safe shelter, education, training and healthcare to help reduce their risk of being exploited and trafficked.
